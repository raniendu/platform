My Story

What drives me? I'm passionate about transforming messy, chaotic data into clear, actionable insights. Whether it's building a streaming pipeline that processes millions of events per second or ensuring analysts can trust their dashboards, I find satisfaction in making data useful. The highlight of my work is collaborating with brilliant, dedicated people who share a commitment to quality.
