Hey, I'm Raniendu

I build data systems that empower teams to make smarter, data-driven decisions. My journey in tech has taken me through some of the industry's biggest names, including Amazon, Meta, and AWS.

Based in the Greater Seattle Area, I spend my days architecting and maintaining robust data platforms. Outside of work, you'll find me exploring new cities or tinkering with personal projects.

My Story

Originally from Jammu, India. I started my career at Teradata in Pune, India. I later joined Amazon in India and at Amazon, I've had the chance to work on a variety of areas such as Transportation Systems, Amazon Web Services, Just Walk Out Technology and Amazon Ads. I briefly worked at Meta in the Ads Monetization team and currently I am back at Amazon working in the Amazon Ads org.

What drives me? I'm passionate about transforming messy, chaotic data into clear, actionable insights. Whether it's building a streaming pipeline that processes millions of events per second or ensuring analysts can trust their dashboards, I find satisfaction in making data useful. The highlight of my work is collaborating with brilliant, dedicated people who share a commitment to quality.
